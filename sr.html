Our Mission

At Rocket.Chip Web Solutions, we believe in giving something back to the community. Our philosophy is 'more than profit'. We have commited to giving 10% of all our quoted contract prices to an Irish charity or social enterprise which works to help those that are disadvantaged or need assistance, in Irish society.
