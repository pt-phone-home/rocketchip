What is Search Engine Optimisation?

Search Engine Optimisation or SEO is the process of getting 'traffic' from natural search results on search engines such as Google or Bing. This is not the same as paid search ads.

SEO is a set of tools and processes that are carried out during the design and development process which increase the likelihood of your business appearing in a users search results, when they go looking for products or services online.

What does SEO involve?
    The SEO process begins at the design stage and continues right throughout the development process:
  • Structuring your content
  • Creating and managing metadata
  • Optimising site performance
  • Integration and management of web analytics
