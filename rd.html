Responsive Design

Responsive design is an approach to web design where the website responds or resizes itself depending on the size of the device it is being viewed on. This means that a website will adjust its overall size, the size of certain components, and/or the layout of components, when it is viewed on a deskop, laptop or mobile device. With the growth of mobile devices, responsive design is essential for any business website, ensuring your content is accessible regardless of where your customer is.
